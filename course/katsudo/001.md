## te-form verb

- something continuous (it's going on)
- verb + temasu/teimasu
- e.g. shigoto wa shiteimasen
- e.g. hoteru de hataraiteimasu
- opera wo benkyoou shiteimasu
- chuugoku ni sundeimasu
