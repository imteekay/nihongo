## Utilizar "e"

- to: boku to anata wa (substantivo)
- soshite: anata wa kirei desu. soshite tasashii desu
- te/de: mise wa yasukute, benri desu (adjetivo)
  - hiroi -> hirokute
  - yasui -> yasukute
  - shizukana -> shizukade
  - nigiyakana -> nigiyakade
  - tanoshii -> tanoshikute
